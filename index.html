Welcome!

Sign-up for the George Clark Memorial Tournament

The Carolina Classics Hockey Club was formed in 2012 with 22 charter members. The new team achieved its initial goal of putting a CCHC team on the ice for the USAH Nationals, compiling a spotless record (didn’t win a damn game!) but enjoying every minute, especially the beer, camaraderie and new friendships!

The members of the CCHC would like to grow this concept into a larger group of players from age 60 to 70+ here in the Carolinas in order to increase the number of opportunities to play in tournaments over a larger age group as well as providing continuity. This will also give CCHC a larger pool of players to facilitate better use of practice ice and grow the “team” aspect among all players of all ages and levels. CCHC will also get into a wider number of great hockey rinks in the process. There are many tournaments up and down the east coast that have divisions for 60+, 65+, and 70+ players. With enough participation, CCHC could have more local games as well.

Feel free to email us for more information.
